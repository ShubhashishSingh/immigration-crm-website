Canada PR Overview

Canada PR is one of the most popular immigration pathways for skilled professionals. Applicants are assessed based on age, education, work experience, language score and adaptability.

Important Requirements

  • Valid passport
  • Education credential assessment
  • IELTS or approved language test
  • Work experience documents
  • Proof of funds

How To Improve Your Chances

Improve your language score, maintain accurate documents, gain skilled work experience and apply through suitable immigration pathways like Express Entry or PNP.
