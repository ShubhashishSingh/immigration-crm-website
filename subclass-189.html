What is Skilled Migration?

Skilled Migration is a pathway for qualified professionals who want to live and work in Australia permanently. It is based on occupation demand, age, education, work experience, English score and points.

Benefits of Skilled Migration

  • Pathway to Australia permanent residency
  • Opportunity to live and work in Australia
  • Access to better career opportunities
  • Family migration possibilities
  • High quality lifestyle
  • Long-term settlement benefits

Documents Required

  • Valid passport
  • Education documents
  • English test score
  • Skills assessment documents
  • Work experience letters
  • Updated CV / Resume
  • Proof of funds
  • Police clearance and medicals
