What is PNP Program?

Provincial Nominee Program (PNP) allows Canadian provinces to nominate skilled workers for permanent residency based on local labour market needs.

Benefits of PNP

  • Higher chances of PR approval
  • Additional CRS points
  • Province-specific immigration pathways
  • Fast processing options
  • Job opportunities in Canada

Documents Required

  • Passport
  • Education documents
  • IELTS / PTE score
  • Work experience letters
  • Resume / CV
  • Proof of funds
  • Police clearance certificate
