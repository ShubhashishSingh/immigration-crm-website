What is CRS Score?

CRS stands for Comprehensive Ranking System. It is a points-based system used in Canada Express Entry to rank candidates for permanent residency.

CRS Score Depends On

  • Age
  • Education qualification
  • English language score
  • Work experience
  • Canadian job offer
  • Provincial nomination
  • Spouse factors, if applicable

Why CRS Score is Important?

A higher CRS score improves your chances of receiving an Invitation to Apply for Canada PR. If your score is low, you can improve it through better IELTS/PTE score, PNP nomination, additional education, or work experience.

Documents Required for CRS Evaluation

  • Passport
  • Education documents
  • IELTS / PTE score
  • Work experience letters
  • Resume / CV
  • Marriage certificate, if applicable
